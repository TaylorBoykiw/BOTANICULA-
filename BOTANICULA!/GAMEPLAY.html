Game Play

The gameplay is based on a point-and-click style that Amanita Design is famous for.

The objective is to save your home tree from the fungus spiders, kinda scary right?

To save your home tree, you must complete multiple puzzels and quests for some of the inhabitants. These puzzels/quests are fairly personal to some of the inhabitants, revealing quite a bit about their lives and the world you live in!

Sometimes the puzzels can be confusing and time consuming- but you shouldn't give up, focus on the overall story and how great it will be after you complete the puzzel

Botanicula is a very quirky, charming little story that anyone can find the time to enjoy.
